1 ABOUT

Hey, I’m Julia!

I'm a native of Atlanta, Georgia currently studying CS and Creative Writing at Stanford. I’m interested in how technology can advance initiatives in healthcare, media, and environmental sustainability, and want to build solutions in these spaces as an operator or investor. All that is to say, I’m still figuring it out :)

Among other things, I have experience building digital health resources for USA Football, writing script coverage for major motion films, and analyzing investment opportunities in the Healthcare space. I love working on personal projects – favorites include a pop-up marketplace supporting female entrepreneurs and this very website.

I am the humble winner of the 2010 Fulton County public library “Summer Splash!” reading competition, in which my competitive nature sparked a lifelong love for stories. I’m hoping to stay true to that passion, hold myself to a New Years resolution (from two years ago…), and put out some new writing on this website.

I love my hobbies, particularly board sports of any variety (snowboarding, skateboarding, surfing, etc.), pickup basketball, and vintage fashion.

Thanks for stopping by and have a lovely day!

Best,
Julia
